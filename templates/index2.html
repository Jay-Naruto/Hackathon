Servigenics

Servigenics is a car service company which brings to you your nearest mechanic, in times of automobile crisis! Whether you need a mechanic or just parts for your vehicle, Servigenics is the right choice! We also provide priority services to mechanics who wish to register on our site and be shown as first priority to customers!

To avail our services follow the steps given below:
1. On our home page you will come across a Login button, press it.
2. If you haven't signed up as a user/ mechanic, click on the sign up link at the bottom.
3. Fill in the relevant details and login to your newly formed account.
4. Go to "Our Services" page and choose whichever service you wish to avail, purchase car parts or Consult a mechanic.
